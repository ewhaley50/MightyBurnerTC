Training Rules Used

Speed / Max V: high intensity, low volume, full recovery; acceleration under 40m, max velocity through fly segments after a build.

Speed Endurance: usually 70–150m reps, 90–100% intensity, with enough recovery to keep quality high.

Specific/Special Endurance: 150–300m+ reps or split runs for race rhythm, lactate tolerance, and finishing capacity. Recovery ranges from incomplete for broken reps to near-complete for high-quality special endurance.

Tempo: lower intensity, higher volume, incomplete recoveries.
