About Mighty Burner

Coaching philosophy

Simple cues, consistent progressions, and reps that translate to race day. We teach athletes how to move — not just how to grind.

Events we cover

Sprints (60–400), hurdles, relays, jumps and general athletic development.

Program Focus

Speed Development

  • Acceleration + top speed mechanics
  • Starts, posture, shin angles
  • Short sprints + quality recovery
  • Strength + mobility basics

Hurdles & Technique

  • Lead/trail leg drills + rhythm
  • Step pattern development
  • Speed with control
  • Race modeling

Coaches

  • Pennsylvania HS State Champion
  • Collegiate athlete – Villanova & Florida State
  • Coach at Northside Christian School

Jeff M

Coach Jeff Mack is a Masters Track & Field World Champion and proud Clearwater, Florida native whose dedication to training has led to record-setting performances on the national and international stage.

  • Masters Track & Field World Champion
  • Speed development specialist
  • Expert in acceleration & block starts
  • Great teacher for beginners and advanced athletes

Coach Jeff is known for his ability to break down sprint mechanics in a clear, simple way. His focus on powerful starts, acceleration phases, and technical consistency helps athletes build confidence and see real improvement quickly.
