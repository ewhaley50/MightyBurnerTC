About Mighty Burner

We coach athletes with a clear plan: fundamentals → speed → confidence.

Coaching philosophy

Simple cues, consistent progressions, and reps that translate to race day. We teach athletes how to move — not just how to grind.

Events we cover

Sprints (60–400), hurdles, relays, and general athletic development. (Customize this list for your team.)

Team culture

Positive energy, accountability, and a supportive environment for beginners and advanced athletes alike.

Programs

Pick what fits your athlete (or we’ll place them).

Speed Development

  • Acceleration + top speed mechanics
  • Starts, posture, shin angles
  • Short sprints + quality recovery
  • Strength + mobility basics

Hurdles & Technique

  • Lead/trail leg drills + rhythm
  • Step pattern development
  • Speed with control
  • Race modeling (age appropriate)

Season Prep (Optional)

  • Build a base without burning out
  • Tempo days + sprint days
  • Injury prevention + recovery habits

Private / Small Group

  • 1:1 or small group sessions
  • Technique breakdowns
  • Customized weekly plan
